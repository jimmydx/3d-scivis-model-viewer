3D Model Viewer for Georeferenced Data

Developed by Jaime Aguilar, Research Scientist at Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University, Daytona Beach, FL. Contact: j.aguilar@erau.edu

This website tool visualizes georeferenced data prepared in MATLAB and Blender, pairing optimized GLB assets with Apple Quick Look USDZ deliverables for AR and mobile review.
